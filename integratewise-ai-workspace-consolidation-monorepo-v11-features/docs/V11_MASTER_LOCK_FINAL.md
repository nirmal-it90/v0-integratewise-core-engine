## Master Headline (Locked)

**Load your work. Store it in your Spine. Think in your IQ Hub. Act through your Cognitive Twin. Govern with your apps.**

---

## v11.0 One-Sentence Thesis

IntegrateWise eliminates Tool Sprawl, solves the CS Team Paradox, and closes the GenAI Divide by normalizing everything into one governed Spine and projecting role-based Views powered by an always-ready Cognitive Twin.

---

## v11.0 — The 5 Core Principles (Locked)

### 1. Keep Your Tools
IntegrateWise doesn't replace Slack, Notion, CRMs, or AI tools. It connects them so your work compounds instead of fragmenting.

### 2. Normalize Once. Render Anywhere (BYOT)
Inputs are normalized into canonical models one time and rendered across Views or external templates without breaking source systems.

### 3. The Spine is the SSOT
All operational data and captured intelligence lives in one governed source of truth with department-aware visibility.

### 4. IQ Hub Compounds Thinking
AI conversations and notes become permanent, searchable work—so knowledge never evaporates into private chat history.

### 5. Governance is Intrinsic
Governor Slack, AI-Relay, Audit Trail, and Policy Gates enforce safety, accountability, and enterprise readiness across every action.

---

## v11.0 Core Loop (Locked)

**Load → Store → Think → Act → Govern**

### Load (Ingestion)
Connectors + Dumps + Slack/Webhooks capture everything without losing context.

### Store (Spine SSOT)
All inputs become canonical entities with provenance, links, and auditability.

### Think (IQ Hub)
Working memory + retrieval turns raw inputs into reusable assets: templates, playbooks, decisions, research.

### Act (Cognitive Twin + Views)
Role-based Views receive prepared insights + suggested actions, with safe write-back proposals.

### Govern (Trust Layer)
AI-Relay, Governor Slack, AuditLog, and Policy Gates control exposure, approvals, and traceability.

## v11.0 First-Time Login (Onboarding — Locked)

1. **Login** (Google / Microsoft / Email)

2. **Persona Analysis** (6–10 seconds) (role + intent routing only)

3. **Persona Insights** (recommended starting View, confirm/override)

4. **Load Your Data** (Connect / Dump / Skip)

5. **Enter Workspace → Today**

## v11.0 Post-Login Daily Loop (Locked)

1. **Login → Today** (Daily Command Center)

2. **Switch into your role-based View**

3. **IQ Hub captures and structures new thinking**

4. **Cognitive Twin prepares context + drafts actions**

5. **Governance logs + gates sensitive operations**
