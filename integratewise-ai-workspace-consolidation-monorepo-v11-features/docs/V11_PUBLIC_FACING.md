## How It Works: The Intelligence Loop

### Load
Capture signals from everywhere: your CRM, calendar, support tools, documents, Slack, webhooks, and file dumps.

### Store
Everything normalizes into the Spine—your single source of truth. Accounts, deals, tasks, interactions, health scores—all unified with full provenance.

### Think
IQ Hub structures your knowledge into reusable assets. Strategies, templates, playbooks, and decisions link to real entities. Your Cognitive Twin retrieves the right context at the right moment.

### Act
Intelligence becomes actionable. Role-based Views surface insights where you work. Suggested actions and drafts appear in context. Safe write-backs execute only with approval.

### Govern
Every action is logged, audited, and controlled. Governor Slack provides real-time oversight. AI-Relay Gateway ensures all AI calls and integrations are tracked. Policy gates enforce security before any commit.
